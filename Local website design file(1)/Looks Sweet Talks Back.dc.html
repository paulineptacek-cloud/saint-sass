05Content Strategy

A chapter-by-chapter build from whisper to sell-out.

The five chapters give the rollout its spine: tease the world, release it chapter by chapter, save the reveal for last, then let the audience write the encore. Curiosity first, conversion second.

07Growth

A hundred thousand followers, earned — not bought.

Follower growth is a lagging metric. It follows reach, and reach follows saves and shares. So the plan optimises for the signals that actually move the account.

0
New Instagram followers over twelve months
0
High-performing pieces per month at 200K+ views
0
View ceiling targeted for signature carousels
01

Chapters as formats

The campaign proves it: serialised content people wait for. Turn chapters into a repeatable carousel format they save and send.

02

Post with consistency

A steady cadence beats sporadic bursts: a reliable weekly rhythm of Reels, carousels and stories keeps the account active, learning, and in front of the algorithm.

03

First-second hooks

Rewrite the opening frame and line as if it's the only thing anyone sees — because on Reels, it usually is.

04

Always-on creator engine

Rolling roster feeding UGC weekly. Amplify the best via collab posts to borrow their audiences.

05

Reply like the brand

Comment sections as content. In-voice replies get pinned, screenshotted and re-shared — free reach.

06

Test, read, repeat

Weekly review of top and bottom posts. Double down on saves and shares; retire what only gets likes.

Proven formats — reels that already work

Five viral mechanics, translated for saint sass.

I studied the best-performing reels in this space. Each one hides a repeatable mechanic — here's how I'd bend it to the campaign.


Be controversial
288 Tsd · reference

Polarise on purpose. A borderline caption is built to be argued about, and the algorithm rewards the fight.

Stand out
179 Tsd · reference

One bold visual, no explanation. 
A loud image that stops the scroll and gets stitched.

Be authentic
124 Tsd · reference

Founder-POV, unfiltered. The "Mom: how was the weekend?" format is native, funny and trusted — exactly saint sass' voice.

Take the community
158 Tsd · reference

Bring them on the trip. "Come with me" diaries turn followers into companions — the girls'-trip chapters, in real time.

Show heritage
577 Tsd · reference

Own where you're from. "Oktoberfest but make it Berlin" flexes German roots — heritage localised, and highly shareable.

The first ninety days

Days 1–30

Audit the last six months, define four to five content pillars, ship three repeatable carousel formats, stand up the creator roster.

Days 31–60

Lock the rollout, launch a consistent weekly posting rhythm, start systematic in-voice community replies.

Days 61–90

Read the data, kill what underperforms, scale the top format, turn the best UGC into always-on collab reach.
